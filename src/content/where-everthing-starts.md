## “Don’t go pro. Just go, first of all.”

was what I came across today while doomscrolling on Instagram. The exact saying in Vietnamese, quoted from a homemade chilly sauce brand owner, was “Không cần pro. Chỉ cần go”. A simple saying in a random chilly sauce PR video somehow gently captured my heart. We indeed all start somewhere. The people social media claims cringe are the one who end up knowing more because they dare to try and dare to fail. The fear is holding me back. But fear from what? Time to move on from overthinking and under-executing to actually making a step first. And this first blog is the proof. An overly simple, going against almost every UX rule I have learned type of blog, but it is just everything I need to start somewhere, right now.
